Experience the grand thrill! Select 3 numbers from 1 to 55. If all three numbers appear among the six drawn numbers, you win the grand prize! Even with just two matches, a return bet is yours. Try your luck with the Grand Lotto today!

6/58 Ultra Lotto

Go ultra with your picks! Choose 3 numbers from 1 to 58. All three matching the six drawn numbers means you win big! If two numbers match, you receive a return bet. The Ultra Lotto offers high stakes and exciting rewards. Play now and be a winner!

2D Lotto

Pick 2 numbers from 1 to 31. You can choose to play straight (S), rumble (R), or pompi (P). Straight wins require exact order, while rumble is any order, and pompi means the same number. It's a quick and fun game with great chances of winning!

3D Lotto

Choose 3 numbers from 0 to 9. Play straight (S) for exact order, rumble (R) for any order, or trio (T) for same numbers. It's a dynamic game with multiple ways to win. Try your luck and see if you can claim a prize!
